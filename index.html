About my work

You're interested? Good!

In this part, I want to tell you some facts about a mobile App I developed in the last year - GlutenCheck. GlutenCheck is avaiabile for iOS and Android and it's totally free. But what's it's purpose?

GlutenCheck helps you manage your celiac desease. It lets you scan barcodes of products and checks the database for the products' ingredients. Afterwads, the App will inform you whether the product conatins gluten.

If you want to know more, visit GlutenCheck.ch!
